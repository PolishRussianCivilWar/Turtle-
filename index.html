Why Turtles Are Important

Turtles play a critical role in marine and terrestrial ecosystems. They help maintain the balance of habitats and are indicators of ecosystem health.

Threats to Turtles

Climate change, pollution, habitat destruction, and poaching are major threats to turtles worldwide. SAVE TURTLES!!!!
